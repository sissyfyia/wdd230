Small Group Dining Area

Our small dining area seats a cozy 20 people for those intimate family gatherings before or after the wedding. Round tables horseshoe around the large rectangular Bride and Groom table. For dates and more details contact our Wedding Coordinator Kory Smith.

Larger Group Dining Option

Our larger room allows for up to 80 guests. This area has long rectangular tables with the Bride and Groom on an elevated square table at the front of the room. Neutral colors will compliment any wedding decor.

Neighboring Reception Hall

Temple Inn & Suites has partnered with Harten Reception Hall next door to accomodate those larger groups of 100 or more. There are many options available for discounts when a room is booked with us.
