Benefit Dinner

A little less than a month ago Manassa lost another one of their brightest and best-Mark Jackson. Jackson Law was one of our premier sponsors. Mark never hesitated to support Manassa Pioneer Days whether it was participating in events or stepping up as a sponsor. Jennifer and her family have also been heavily involved in Pioneer Days from running the Saddle Club to ordering the food for the hamburger stand to building floats and a million things in between. Let's show that same support to Mark and Jennifer's family during this difficult time. Please come eat at the potato bar benefit dinner/auction, donate something to be auctioned, come buy something at the auction.
