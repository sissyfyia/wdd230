Come Discover Manassa

History

Manassa has a very rich history of Spanish settlers and Pioneers from the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who settled the area. With help from the Manassa Project and the LDS Family History Center there are many resources available to find more deep history.

Jack Dempsey

The name that brought Manassa fame is none other than Jack Dempsey the American world heavyweight boxing champion and boxing Hall of Fame Member. Jack was born in Manassa on June 24, 1895 and always held the town in high regard throughout his life. Small beginnings lead to great endings.

Manassa Pioneer Days

Manassa Pioneer Days Celebration is something you don't want to miss. This small and sleep town more than doubles in size with visitors for the weekend long celebration.
